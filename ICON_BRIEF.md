# App Icon Brief — Pedestal: Speech Coach

## Concept
A stylized podium/pedestal shape with a microphone element, communicating public speaking coaching at a glance.

## Design Specifications

### Shape
- A simplified 3-step podium silhouette (winner's podium shape)
- Center step tallest, two shorter steps flanking
- A small microphone icon or sound wave arc above the center step
- Rounded corners on all shapes for a modern, friendly feel

### Colors
- **Background**: Solid forest green (#2D5A3D — Primary brand color)
- **Icon/Symbol**: White (#FFFFFF)
- No gradients, no shadows — flat design for clarity at small sizes

### Size & Format
- 1024x1024px master file (Apple requirement)
- Must be recognizable at 29x29px (smallest iOS size)
- No text in the icon — brand name not needed
- No transparency (Apple rejects transparent icons)

### Android Adaptive Icon
- **Foreground**: White podium + mic symbol, centered in safe zone (66% of 108dp)
- **Background**: Solid #2D5A3D
- **Monochrome**: Same podium shape for Material You theming

### Style References
- Clean, geometric, minimal
- Similar simplicity to Headspace (circle), Calm (leaf), or Duolingo (owl)
- Should feel premium and professional, not playful
