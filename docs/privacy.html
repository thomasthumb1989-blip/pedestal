Privacy Policy

Pedestal: Speech Coach — Effective 24 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Pedestal: Speech Coach ("Pedestal", "the App", "we", "us") handles your information. Pedestal is developed by Dan, based in Wales, United Kingdom.

1. Information We Collect

Pedestal is designed with privacy in mind. We collect as little data as possible:

  • Audio Recordings: When you use the speech analysis feature, your audio is sent to OpenAI's speech-to-text service for transcription. Audio is processed in real time and is not stored on OpenAI's servers after processing is complete.
  • Session Analytics: Your practice sessions, clarity scores, words-per-minute, filler word counts, and transcripts are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device.
  • Subscription Status: If you subscribe, your subscription is managed by RevenueCat. RevenueCat processes your subscription status and anonymous app user ID. Payment is handled entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play Store.

2. What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or personal contact information.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or share any data with advertisers.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs beyond subscription management.
  • We do not store your audio recordings on our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

Pedestal uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI — for speech-to-text transcription. Audio is sent securely via API and is not retained after processing. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat — for subscription management. RevenueCat receives an anonymous user identifier and subscription status. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage

All session data (recordings, transcripts, scores, progress) is stored locally on your device. We do not operate backend servers that store your personal data. If you uninstall the app, all local data is permanently deleted.

5. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You can delete all of your data at any time by going to Settings → Delete All Data within the app. This permanently removes all sessions, progress, and preferences from your device.

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Data portability

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we do not maintain user accounts, you have full control over your data at all times.

6. Children's Privacy

Pedestal is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
