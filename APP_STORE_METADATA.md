Pedestal closes that gap. Every diagnosis comes with a targeted drill. Every session builds on the last. Your progress is tracked automatically so you can see real improvement over days and weeks.

## 10. Review Notes (for App Store Review Team)

```
Pedestal is a speech coaching app. Users record themselves speaking, and the app transcribes and analyzes their speech using OpenAI's Whisper API. No account is required.

To test:
1. Open the app and complete the 3-screen onboarding
2. Start the free trial on the paywall screen (tap "Try free for 3 days")
3. Tap the record button on the Practice tab
4. Speak for 20+ seconds, then tap stop
5. View your results with clarity score and feedback

The app requires microphone permission to record speech. Audio is sent to OpenAI for transcription and is not stored after processing. A consent modal explains this before the first recording.

No login or account creation is required.
```
